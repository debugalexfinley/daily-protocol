## Dopamine Reset Protocol Summary

The **Reset Phase** is a structured reboot of the dopaminergic system. It combines heavy lifting, rucking, targeted peptides, and specific nootropics to restore sensitivity, clear tolerance, and rebuild baseline drive and motivation.

### Goals
- Reset dopamine receptor sensitivity (especially D1/D2 after prolonged stimulant or high-dopamine use)
- Restore BDNF levels via Semax + 9-me-BC
- Repair gut and systemic tissue with BPC-157 (3–4× daily, oral)
- Rebuild circadian rhythm via Pinealon + light discipline (Golden Glo glasses, Chroma Nightshades)
- Reestablish anabolic baseline via GH + GHK-Cu + Retatrutide

### Key Reset-Phase Compounds
| Compound | Dose | Role |
|---|---|---|
| 9-me-BC | 20mg AM | Dopamine synthesis upregulation (DAT + TH enzymes) |
| BPC-157 | 500mcg 3–4×/day oral | Gut + systemic repair |
| Semax | 300mcg nasal 1–2×/day | BDNF boost, ACTH analog, focus |
| Selank | 250mcg nasal 1–2×/day | Anxiolytic, anxiety dampening without sedation |
| Pinealon | 1mg oral fasted | Circadian + epigenetic regulation |
| Bromantane | 100mg sublingual | Dopamine synthesis (DAT reuptake + TH enzyme) |
| Alpha-GPC | 600mg AM (lift) / Citicoline 500mg (ruck) | Choline substrate |
| Cordyceps | 1000mg 2×/day | Mitochondrial energy, cycling required |
| Polygala Tenuifolia | 100mg 2–3×/day | BDNF, dopamine potentiation |
| Agmatine Sulfate | 750mg–1500mg/day | Tolerance reset via NMDA modulation |

### Reset-Phase Rules
- **9-me-BC**: Max 4 weeks (28 days), then stop — not a cycling compound, requires full clearance
- **BPC-157**: 28 days on / 14 days off
- **Semax**: No dosing after 3 PM (disrupts sleep)
- **No Sabroxy / Dynamine** during reset (reserve focus stacks for maintenance phase)
- **No nuclear stacks** during reset — let the system recover
- **Alpha-GPC**: Lift days only (depletes choline stores) — use Citicoline on ruck/rest days
- **Chroma Golden Glo glasses** at wake (lift days), removed at sunrise/outdoor activity
- **Chroma Nightshades** at sunset daily

### Physical Protocol (Reset Phase)
- **Lift days** (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri): Heavy compounds 5:30 AM → Ruck 6:30 AM post-lift
- **Ruck days** (Wed, Sat, Sun): Fasted ruck 6:15 AM, 20–30 lb plate carrier, sunrise exposure
- **Intra-workout**: 1 scoop intra carb (156 cal, 39g carb) — lift days only

### Peptide Protocol (Both Phases)

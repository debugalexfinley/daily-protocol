## Protocol Overview

Two phases:

- **RESET** — Active dopamine reset with BPC-157 (oral), 9-me-BC, Semax/Selank multiple doses/day. More aggressive cycling, stricter timing.
- **MAINTENANCE (POST)** — All supplements once in the AM. Simplified. Sabroxy/Dynamine as-needed for deep work.

**Daily constants (both phases):**
- Retatrutide ~1.33mg SubQ Mon/Wed/Fri (4mg/week split)
- GH 2 IU SubQ daily before bed
- GHK-Cu 2mg SubQ Mon–Fri before bed
- Lights out by 10:15 PM
